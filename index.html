I've spent 15+ years at Renaissance Learning designing products used by teachers, students, and administrators across K–12 education. My focus has shifted over time from individual features to the systems that connect them, defining the patterns, standards, and structures that let design scale across a complex, interconnected product suite.

I believe a designer's job is to make good decisions possible, both for the team, and for the users they serve. That means facilitating an inclusive process, bringing together design, product, and engineering around shared goals. For users — teachers and administrators constantly adapting tools to fit their classrooms and students — it means providing the right building blocks so they can design the solutions that work best for them.

I'm based in Portland, Oregon, and am comfortable working remotely or in person. View my resume and portfolio, or reach me at singermail@gmail.com or on LinkedIn.
